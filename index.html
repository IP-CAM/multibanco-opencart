Ifthenpay module for OpenCart

This is the Ifthenpay module for OpenCart CMS

Multibanco is one Portuguese payment method that allows the customer to pay by bank reference. This plugin will allow you to generate a payment Reference that the customer can then use to pay for his order on the ATM or Home Banking service. This plugin uses one of the several gateways/services available in Portugal, IfthenPay, and a contract with this company is required. See more at Ifthenpay.
